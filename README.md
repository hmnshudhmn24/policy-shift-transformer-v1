# POLICY SHIFT TRANSFORMER V1

POLICY SHIFT TRANSFORMER V1 is a Transformer-based NLP model designed to forecast potential future government policy changes by learning patterns from historical policy documents, official reports, and related news articles.

This project is built to be fully compatible with Hugging Face, modular, and easy to fine-tune or extend.

Government policies often evolve gradually. However, identifying early signals of future policy shifts is difficult and time-consuming for policy analysts, researchers, think tanks, law professionals, and competitive exam aspirants.

POLICY SHIFT TRANSFORMER V1 aims to predict likely policy direction or shifts based on textual evidence.
